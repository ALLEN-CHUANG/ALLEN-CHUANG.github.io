Hi, I am Allen.
I am a prospective Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering. In the past, I have been fortunate enough to work with Prof. Ming-Feng Tsai and Prof. Chuan-Ju Wang as a master's student at National ChengChi University and collaborate with Prof. Yuan Fang and Prof. Ee-Peng Lim as a visiting student at Living Analytics Research Centre in Singapore. I received my B.S. in Mathematics in 2017 and my M.S. in Computer Science in 2020, both from National ChengChi University.

My research interests include recommender systems via modeling user behaviors and sparse labeling textual data. I am also interested in applying graph representation to several domains, e.g., social network analysis. Recently, I have worked on exploring user preference’s probability distribution and analyzing text network in recommender systems.
